Everything you need

One click, and the page is your canvas

No account, no setup. Click the icon and the toolbar appears — right where you're already looking.

Every tool you'd want

Pen, highlighter, boxes, ellipses, arrows, lines and text — with adjustable thickness, opacity, custom colors and an eyedropper.

Blur before you share

Hide emails, names or card numbers with the redact tool, and drop numbered pins that count themselves for step-by-step guides.

Save it or copy it

Save a PNG, copy straight to the clipboard, or export the whole scrolling page as one image. Your markup can come back next visit.

Laser pointer

A glowing pointer for live presentations and screen recordings — nothing to draw, just guide the eye.

Keyboard shortcuts

Alt+A to open, single keys to switch tools — and you can remap every one of them to whatever fits your hands.

Private by design

No standing access to any site, no tracking, no accounts. It only runs after you click, and never reads or sends the pages you visit.

🛡️

Your pages stay yours

Most annotation extensions ask to read and change data on every site you visit. This one doesn't — it has no standing access to anything, and nothing ever leaves your device automatically.
